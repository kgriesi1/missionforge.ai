Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Last updated: April 2026

Who We Are

Mission Forge AI is an AI strategy and enterprise consulting firm serving organizations across public sector, private enterprise, nonprofit, and startup, united by a mission to make the world better, safer, and more just. Our website is located at missionforge.ai. You can reach us through our contact page.

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to respond to your inquiry or improve how the site functions. This may include:

Contact information you voluntarily submit through our contact form: your name, email address, organization, phone number, and the message you write.

Basic analytics data (if you consent), such as pages visited and general geographic region. This data is aggregated and not linked to your identity.

Standard server log data such as IP address and browser type, which is retained briefly for security purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use your contact information solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We will never add you to a mailing list without your explicit consent.

Cookies

This site uses two categories of cookies:

Essential cookies are required for the site to function. They remember your cookie consent preference and support basic navigation. These are always active.

Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it. These are only set if you choose "Accept All" in the cookie banner. You can opt out at any time by clearing your browser's local storage for this domain, or by using your browser's privacy settings.

Your Cookie Choices

When you first visit this site, you are presented with a banner giving you the choice to accept all cookies or accept essential cookies only. Your preference is saved in your browser's local storage and honored on future visits.

To change your preference, clear the "mf_cookie_consent" entry from your browser's local storage (found in your browser's developer tools under Application > Local Storage), then reload the page.

Third-Party Services

Our contact form is processed by Formspree, a third-party form service. Submissions are transmitted securely to Formspree's servers and forwarded directly to our team. Formspree's own privacy policy governs their handling of submitted data.

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to conduct business. You may request deletion of your information at any time through our contact page.

Your Rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have rights under applicable privacy law (including GDPR and CCPA) to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you, or to object to certain uses. To exercise any of these rights, use our contact page. We will respond within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

Questions or concerns about this policy? Reach out through our contact page.
