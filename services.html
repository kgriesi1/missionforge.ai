04

Fractional CPO / CTO / CAIO

Not every organization can, or should, hire three full-time executives. But navigating AI today demands the perspective of all three: a product visionary, a technology architect, and an AI strategist who keeps it all aligned to mission. Mission Forge provides each of these, fractionally, with the depth and accountability of a full-time principal.

CPO: Chief Product Officer

The CPO owns product vision, strategy, and roadmap. They bridge constituent needs with technology capabilities, ensuring that what gets built is what the mission actually requires. For organizations launching new digital products, rethinking existing ones, or struggling to prioritize against mission goals, a Fractional CPO brings focus and experience to move fast without moving wrong.

CTO: Chief Technology Officer

The CTO owns the technology architecture, engineering leadership, infrastructure decisions, and technical direction of the organization. For organizations that have outgrown their current foundation, or haven't yet built one worth scaling, a Fractional CTO provides the strategic clarity and hands-on guidance to get there without the full-time overhead.

CAIO: Chief AI Officer

The CAIO is the executive responsible for AI strategy across the organization, not just which tools to use, but how AI adoption is governed, how risk is managed, and how the organization ensures that AI remains aligned to its values and mission. As AI becomes central to how organizations operate, the CAIO is the executive who makes sure it doesn't drift. Most organizations don't need a full-time CAIO yet. All of them need the thinking one brings.

What This Looks Like
Defined weekly or monthly engagement structure scaled to your needs
Participation in executive leadership and board meetings
Product, technology, and AI strategy ownership
Team mentorship and engineering leadership
Vendor negotiation and platform evaluation
AI governance and responsible adoption aligned to organizational values

Recruiting and onboarding a CPO, CTO, and Chief AI Officer can take 6 to 18 months and exceed $1.5M per year in combined compensation. A fractional team delivers proven executive leadership in days, scaled to your mission and your budget.
