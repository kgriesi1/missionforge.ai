Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

Do you only work with large organizations?

Not at all. We've led teams of 225 people and helped individual founders think through their first technology hire. What matters is that your work is mission-driven and that we can add genuine value, not headcount.

We're a small nonprofit. Can we afford this?

Mission drives engagement structure. We work with organizations across a wide range of budget realities, and we're committed to scoping engagements that deliver real return. Start the conversation, and we'll be honest about fit.

What if we're not sure what kind of help we need?

That's the most common starting point. A discovery conversation is designed exactly for this. We'll help you name what's really going on before any services are discussed.

Do you work with secular organizations?

Yes. Our work is guided by values-driven leadership, but Mission Forge AI serves any organization where purpose and people come before profit, regardless of religious affiliation.

How quickly can we get started?

We are intentional about the engagements we take on, which means capacity is limited at any given time. Reach out as early as possible, even if your start date is months away.
